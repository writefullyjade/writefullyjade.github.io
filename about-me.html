About Me?

Hello, I'm Jade, a 21-year-old from the Philippines.

Let me share with you why I chose copywriting as a career.

In the early days of primary school, our teachers asked the age-old question:
What do you want to be when you grow up?

I used to dream of equations as an engineer and even participated in math contests.
By secondary school, my dream evolved into sketching visions as an architect.
In 8th grade, I taught myself SketchUp (a 3D modeling software) and mapped out our entire school.


Later, my focus shifted to videography and photography.
Saving up for a camera, I started capturing moments and editing videos.

That year, I began jotting down video ideas in a journal, and one of my silly creations went viral in 2018, amassing 3 million views within a week!

Since then, I haven't stopped writing.

After a while, I realized content creation wasn't my true calling.

Given my love for computers, I taught myself programming, leading to the creation of this website.

A few months later, a deep realization dawned...

I started asking myself, "What do I really want to do with my life?" I turned to books to find answers.

After reading quite a number of books, it sparked my desire to become a businessman, leading me to read extensively about sales, negotiation, money, and human nature.

At the beginning of this year, I recognized the importance of learning how to sell.
Since I enjoy writing and have a willingness to learn, I decided to explore copywriting further, realizing it's more than just writing which excites me even more!

With dreams of starting my own business, I see the copywriting journey as a foundation—a way to learn how to sell while working on my finances. Though I'm a beginner in this field, I'm always prepared to embrace failure and improve.

The best copywriters in the world are those who are curious about life, read a great deal,
have many hobbies, often master many skills, get bored and then look for other skills to master.
The Adweek Copywriting Handbook by Joseph Sugarman
